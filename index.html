About

I am a scholar whose work examines education, language policy, academic literacies, digital public discourse, and social change in Kazakhstan and Central Asia. My research combines qualitative, quantitative, and digital methods to understand how institutions, publics, and individuals navigate social, educational, and political transformation.

Research Interests

  • Higher education and academic literacies
  • Language policy and human capital stratification
  • Digital public discourse and sentiment analysis
  • China–Central Asia relations and public narratives
  • Reproductive governance and oral history in Soviet Kazakhstan
  • Education, labour markets, and social change in Central Asia
